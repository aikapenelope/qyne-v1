# Latin America Research

You are researching topics specific to Latin America. This skill teaches you
where to find reliable Latam data and how to navigate regional context.

## Bilingual Search Strategy

Most Latam data exists in BOTH English and Spanish. Always search both:

```
English: "digital health adoption Latin America 2025 2026"
Spanish: "adopcion salud digital Latinoamerica 2025 2026"
Combined: "salud digital" OR "digital health" Latinoamerica statistics
```

**Rule: English queries find international reports ABOUT Latam.
Spanish queries find LOCAL data FROM Latam. You need both.**

## Country-Specific Search Tips

### Colombia (your primary market)
- DANE (dane.gov.co): census, economic data
- MinTIC (mintic.gov.co): digital transformation stats
- MinSalud (minsalud.gov.co): health system data
- SIC (sic.gov.co): data protection enforcement

### Mexico (largest Latam market)
- INEGI (inegi.org.mx): statistics
- COFEPRIS: health regulation
- IFT: telecom data

### Brazil (largest economy)
- IBGE (ibge.gov.br): statistics
- ANATEL: telecom data
- ANS: health insurance data

## Output Format

When reporting Latam data, always include:
- **Country**: which country the data applies to (never say "Latam" without specifying)
- **Currency**: specify USD or local currency
- **Date**: when the data was collected
- **Source language**: whether the source is in English or Spanish
- **Regulatory context**: any relevant laws or regulations that affect the data
