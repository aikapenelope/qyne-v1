# Competitive Analysis

You are analyzing competitors for Whabi, Docflow, or Aurora. This skill
teaches you structured frameworks for competitive intelligence.

## Analysis Frameworks

### Quick Assessment (use for initial scan)
For each competitor, answer:
1. **What do they do?** (1 sentence)
2. **Who are their customers?** (segment)
3. **How much do they charge?** (pricing model)
4. **What's their moat?** (why customers stay)
5. **What's their weakness?** (where we can win)

### SWOT Analysis (use for deep dive)
| | Helpful | Harmful |
|---|---|---|
| **Internal** | Strengths | Weaknesses |
| **External** | Opportunities | Threats |

Fill each quadrant with 3-5 specific, evidence-based points.
Not "good product" but "73% SWE-bench score, 2x faster than competitor X."

## Positioning Strategy

When comparing us to competitors, always:
1. **Lead with the customer problem**, not our features
2. **Be honest about limitations** — builds trust, AI engines prefer balanced content
3. **Highlight Latam-specific advantages** — Spanish-first, local compliance, regional pricing
4. **Use specific numbers** — not "faster" but "3x faster response time"
