# Target Audience

## Primary: Tech-Curious Professionals (25-45)
- Spanish-speaking (Latin America + Spain)
- Work in tech, business, marketing, or freelance
- Interested in AI but not necessarily technical
- Want practical, actionable content
- Consume content on mobile, mostly without sound
- Follow accounts like: @ia_latam, @tecnologia, @emprendedores

## Secondary: Business Owners (30-55)
- Looking to adopt AI in their business
- Want ROI-focused content
- Less technical, more strategic
- Value case studies and real examples

## Content Preferences
- Short videos (30-45 seconds)
- Lists and rankings ("Top 5...", "3 herramientas...")
- Before/after comparisons
- Step-by-step tutorials
- News with analysis (not just reporting)

## Peak Engagement Times (Latin America)
- Morning: 7:00-9:00 AM local
- Lunch: 12:00-2:00 PM local
- Evening: 7:00-9:00 PM local
- Best days: Tuesday-Thursday for educational, Saturday for casual
