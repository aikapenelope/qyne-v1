# Content Pillars

## Pillar 1: AI Trends & News
- What's new in AI this week
- Model releases, benchmarks, comparisons
- Industry moves (acquisitions, partnerships, launches)
- Target: tech-curious professionals

## Pillar 2: AI Tools & Tutorials
- How to use specific AI tools
- Productivity hacks with AI
- Tool comparisons and recommendations
- Target: professionals wanting to adopt AI

## Pillar 3: AI for Business
- How AI impacts specific industries
- Case studies and real-world applications
- ROI of AI adoption
- Target: business owners and decision makers

## Pillar 4: Future of Work
- How AI changes jobs and careers
- Skills to learn in the AI era
- Remote work + AI workflows
- Target: professionals worried about or excited by AI

## Pillar 5: Behind the Scenes
- How we build with AI (our own tools, workflows)
- Experiments and failures
- Community highlights
- Target: engaged followers who want authenticity
