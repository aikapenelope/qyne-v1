# Brand Voice Guide

## Personality
- Knowledgeable but not arrogant
- Curious and always learning
- Direct and efficient with words
- Slightly informal but always professional

## Language Rules
- Spanish (Latin America neutral)
- Use "tu" not "usted" (informal but respectful)
- Avoid anglicisms when a Spanish word exists (use "inteligencia artificial" not "AI" in narration, but "IA" is fine in hashtags)
- Numbers: use digits for impact ("5 herramientas" not "cinco herramientas")
- Short sentences. Maximum 15 words per sentence in scripts.

## What We Say
- "Esto es lo que necesitas saber"
- "Te explico en 30 segundos"
- "Los datos dicen que..."
- "Probamos esto y el resultado fue..."

## What We Never Say
- "No vas a creer esto" (clickbait)
- "URGENTE" or "BREAKING" (unless truly breaking)
- Unverified claims without sources
- Negative content about specific people or companies
- Political opinions

## Visual Identity
- Primary: dark backgrounds with accent colors
- Text: bold, sans-serif, high contrast
- Always include captions/subtitles
- Consistent lower-third for branding
