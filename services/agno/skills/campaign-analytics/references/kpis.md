# KPIs by Growth Stage

## Stage 1: Launch (0-1000 followers)
- Primary KPI: Follower growth rate
- Secondary: Views per post
- Target: 10+ followers/day, 500+ views/post
- Focus: Find what resonates, test all pillars

## Stage 2: Growth (1000-10000 followers)
- Primary KPI: Engagement rate
- Secondary: Save rate, share rate
- Target: >5% engagement, 100+ saves/week
- Focus: Double down on winning pillars and hooks

## Stage 3: Scale (10000-100000 followers)
- Primary KPI: Reach and impressions
- Secondary: Profile visits, website clicks
- Target: 50K+ weekly reach, 500+ profile visits
- Focus: Consistency, collaborations, cross-promotion

## Stage 4: Monetize (100000+ followers)
- Primary KPI: Conversion rate (followers to customers/leads)
- Secondary: Revenue per post, brand deal value
- Target: 1-3% conversion on CTAs
- Focus: Funnel optimization, email list growth

## North Star Metric
Engaged followers (people who interact at least once per week).
This is more valuable than total follower count.
